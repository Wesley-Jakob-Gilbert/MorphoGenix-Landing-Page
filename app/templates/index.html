How it works

Three sensors. One retainer. Your whole oral posture, live.

A thermoformed dental shell with a sealed array of pressure sensors streams over BLE to your phone at 10Hz.

01
Wear the device

A custom-fit upper-arch retainer with a sealed FSR array on the palate and molars. Biocompatible silicone. Barely there.

02
Stream over BLE

An nRF52840 MCU transmits [t, palate_L, palate_R, occlusal] over Nordic UART at 10Hz. Zero wires.

03
See your posture

A live tongue-pressure heatmap, a 0–100 Posture Score, and session history. Fix what you can finally see.

The science

What we actually measure.

Your palate is the immovable mountain your face is sculpted around. Bone remodels under pressure — and until now there was no way to quantify the forces you apply to it every waking and sleeping hour. MorphoGenix reads those forces directly.

Tongue posture (mewing)
Bilateral palatal pressure > 0.5 N sustained > 2 s.
Bruxism & clenching
Occlusal FSR spike > 2 N lasting > 0.5 s.
Mouth breathing
Absence of palatal pressure > 30 s during sleep.
